# Amazon Global Sales Analysis | Power BI Project

I built an interactive dashboard analyzing multi-year Amazon global sales data to extract business insights — not just visuals.

 ## What I Analyzed:
* Year-wise Sales Performance (2012–2015)\
* Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)\
*  Market-wise distribution (APAC, Europe, USCA, LATAM, Africa)\
* Regional Sales Mapping\
* Top 5 & Bottom 5 Profit Products\
* Profit by Customer

## Key Insights:
 * Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue (~51%)\
 * Asia Pacific leads overall market share (~32%)\
 * Few products generate majority of profit (clear Pareto pattern)\
 * Certain products consistently underperform despite sales volume\
 * Profit concentration exists among limited high-value customers

## Tools Used:
* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* DAX for KPIs
*  Data Modeling

### This project helped me practice:
 * Turning raw sales data into decision-ready insights\
 * Identifying profit leakages\
 * Highlighting high-performing markets and products\
 * Building executive-level KPI dashboards

### If this were a real business scenario, actions I’d recommend:
 * Double down on high-profit products
 * Re-evaluate low-margin SKUs
 * Focus marketing on high-performing regions
 * Improve retention strategies for top customers
